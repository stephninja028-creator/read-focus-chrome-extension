Read Focus - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Read Focus is a browser extension designed to help users focus on reading by providing a distraction-free environment. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. All data remains on your computer and is never sent to our servers or any third parties.

Local Data Storage

The extension may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Reading highlights and notes: Text you highlight and notes you create are stored locally in your browser's storage
  • Extension settings: Your preferences for the extension's behavior
  • Session data: Temporary data to maintain the extension's functionality during your browsing session

Permissions

Read Focus requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your highlights and settings locally
  • Active Tab: To access the current webpage for reading enhancement
  • Scripting: To inject reading enhancement features into web pages
  • All URLs: To work on any website you visit

Third-Party Services

Read Focus does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or tracking systems.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system updated for optimal security.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's update notes and on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the Read Focus extension, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Compliance

This extension complies with Chrome Web Store policies and does not violate user privacy. All functionality is transparent and user-controlled.
